Biography

I am a second-year Ph.D. student at the Institute for AI Industry Research (AIR), Tsinghua University, co-supervised by Prof. Weiying Ma and Prof. Hao Zhou. I received my B.S. degree from Department of Computer Science and Technology and my double B.A. degree from Department of Philosophy, Tsinghua University in July, 2022.

My research interest includes Deep Generative Models and their applications in Drug Design.
