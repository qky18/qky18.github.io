Biography

Hi! I am a second-year Ph.D. student at the Institute for Artificial Industry Research, Tsinghua University, co-supervised by Prof. Weiying Ma and Prof. Hao Zhou. I received my B.S. degree from Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University, together with my double B.A. degree from Department of Philosophy in July, 2022.

My research interest includes deep generative models, and their applications in Drug Design.
